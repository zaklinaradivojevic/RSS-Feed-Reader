# Product Definition: Frontpage

## What

Frontpage is a customizable content aggregator that pulls RSS and Atom feeds into a single, well-designed reading dashboard. Users add feeds from their favorite sources — dev blogs, design publications, newsletters, changelogs — and browse everything in one organized interface.

## Who

Developers, designers, and tech professionals who want one place to keep up with blogs, newsletters, changelogs, and publications they care about.

**User profiles:**

- **The busy developer** who follows 20+ blogs but can't keep up. Wants a quick "what did I miss?" view and the ability to save things for later.
- **The design-conscious reader** who cares about how their tools look and feel. Wants a reading experience that's pleasant, not utilitarian.
- **The team lead / generalist** who tracks content across frontend, backend, design, DevOps, and AI. Needs category organization to manage breadth.
- **The newsletter hoarder** who subscribes to everything but reads nothing. Wants a better way to triage and prioritize content.

## Why

Keeping up with tech content is scattered — dozens of blogs, newsletters, release notes spread across bookmarks, email inboxes, Twitter threads, and half-remembered links. There's no calm, organized home for everything you want to read.

Frontpage solves this by giving users a personalized front page for tech content. One place. Clean design. Your sources, your categories, your pace.

## Core Value Proposition

**"Your personalized front page for tech content."**

## What Makes This a Strong Product Challenge

- **Incredible guest experience** — Pre-loaded with real content from curated feeds. Visitors are _using the product_ the moment they try it as guest. This is what makes the project shareable — anyone clicking your link sees the product, not a login wall.
- **Rich design surface** — Layout system, card designs, reading view, navigation, responsive behavior, onboarding — huge room for taste and creativity.
- **Natural engineering complexity** — RSS parsing, CORS handling, data normalization, caching, performance optimization. Genuine web engineering, not artificial.
- **Varied solutions guaranteed** — Layout choices, navigation patterns, digest design, onboarding — every submission will look different.
- **Broad community appeal** — Content about development AND design. Everyone consumes online content.
